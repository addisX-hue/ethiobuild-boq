ምንድነው? / What is it?

AddisBOQ የኢትዮጵያ የግንባታ ፕሮጀክቶች ወጪ ለማስላት እና የስራ ዝርዝር (BOQ) ለማዘጋጀት የተሰራ ድረ-ገጽ እና መተግበሪያ ነው።

AddisBOQ started as a cost calculator and BOQ generator for Ethiopian construction projects — and has grown into a small suite of tools for engineers, contractors, and site teams, from initial cost estimation through to daily site management.
